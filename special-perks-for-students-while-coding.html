Github Student Developer Pack

Now, If you are a student who loves to code and you are a developer or want to be a developer, this will help you. Github Student Developer Pack: You can opt for the Github Education pack and get a ton of paid software for free. Jetbrains For Education

You can use the paid version of Jetbrains software such as Pycharm for free using the Jetbrains Educational Licenses.

Microsoft Office 365 Education

If you’re a student or teacher, you are eligible to get the online version of Microsoft Office for free, plus 1 TB of online storage.

Youth Entertainment Offers on Amazon Prime can help you save big bucks. After you join paid Prime membership and complete payment, you will need to verify your age within 15 days of sign-up to avail 50% off.
